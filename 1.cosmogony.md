# The Analysis of the Cosmogenesis by the Seers and Sages

This text bears witness to the Analysis of Cosmogenesis by the Seers and Sages,
who wished to provide a testimony to preserve the result of their noble science
for future generations. May any who alters or corrupts these words be cursed by
Altracea forever, and may their forms dissolve back into the Great Nothing.

Behold the Analysis of the Seers and Sages:

## The Zeroth Age

Before the beginning, there was the Great Nothing, an infinite potential that
contained every possible Dyad and every possible Annihilation. Yet it had no
Form. Only once the infinitessimal Singularity, called by some the Great Unity,
arose did the universe truly begin. As the emanations of the Singularity spread
out over the Great Nothing, the reverberations brought forth Dyads. The Dyads
vibrated in an ecstatic mixture of love and war, birth and death and rebirth,
and their children sired the fields and forces. The nubile universe could not
contain this great Divine Orgy, and thus the cosmos flexed, bent, and expanded.
Once the universe had expanded sufficiently, light and matter, the descendants
of the Dyads, cooled and solidified. Thus began Time and ended the Zeroth Age.

## The First Age

In the beginning, before the first shadows coagulated, the universe found its
boundaries, which though imperceptible to those within it nevertheless produced
reflections of the primordial vibrations. These vibrations moved and shaped the
Descendants of the Dyads, setting Forms in motion which became the Celestials --
the Stars, Planets, Moons, and minor Bodies. By the end of the First Age, the
Descendants of the Dyads had witnessed the births and deaths of a trillion Stars.

## The Second Age

In the Second Age, the Stars and Planets had stabilized into an orderly dance,
and this dance inspired the Descendants of the Dyads to produce Life. Life was
at first very simple. But the primordial vibrations reflected once more and
swept through the universe, this time leaving setting in motion new Forms that
combined into ever more complex arrangements. One of these combinations led to
the evolution of complex Life, but another of these combinations led to the
evolution of complex Morphic Resonances. In the birthplace of humanity, several
of these Morphic Resonances took up residence among our Celestials.

When our ancesters first began to dream, these Morphic Resonances took notice.
They had never before seen such beings, and their curiosity led to further
combinations and evolutions. For millions of years, these Morphic Resonances
mingled with our ancestors and guided them, and thus arose the Old Gods and the
First Humans.

## The Third Age

In the Third Age, the Old Gods directed the First Humans to build great temples
and monuments. They co-created farming, culture, and the seeds of civilization.
And then, one day, the Old Gods withdrew from humanity. It was said that they
grew weary of the noise of the First Humans and retired to their Celestials.
When the last of the First Humans who heard his God ceased to hear the Voice,
all was for a time lost -- the societies of bronze were overtaken by the Sea
Peoples. Thus began a time of chaos that threatened to snuff out humanity, but
our ancestors eventually found their footing without the Gods.

## The Fourth Age

The Fourth Age was a time of material pursuits and technological progress for
its own sake. The godless humans found no higher purpose than their many
insatiable appetites, and so they engorged themselves. Many wars were fought and
many millions died, and yet billions more arose. It was then that the humans
began meddling with the Celestial abodes of the Old Gods. The Old Gods could not
remain hidden, and so they revealed themselves through their Avatars.
